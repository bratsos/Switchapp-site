Hello fellow gamer

This is not a commercial app that solves an actual problem or makes the world a better place. This is just an side/educational project of two developers.

You probably are a gamer. We are too. You probably wasted a lot of hours on Twitch.tv watching others playing your favorite game. But you don't enjoy every streamer the same right?

Well now you can organize your favorite streamers on an app and be notified when they go online, based on the rating you give them from 1 to 3.
